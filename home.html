Project Bio

We are a group of six students studying at the University if Wollongong that has banded together to make "Cultural Pentagon".

Our name "Cultural Pentagon" may be confusing as there are six of us, however our name shows the five different cultures (Japan, Vietnam, China, Phillippines, Australia) that we represent.
